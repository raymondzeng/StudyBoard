Welcome to StudyBoard, an online study tool developed by four Stuyvesant High School seniors, none of whom are extraordinarily good-looking young women..

StudyBoard currently has a live whiteboard and a studysheet uploading system. The whiteboard system can be used to draw notes and share them in real time. It is complemented with a live chat feature that can be used for discussion.

The Studysheet system can also be used to upload study sheets and share them with the world. If you are logged in, you will receive credit for authoring the study sheet, and your beautiful name will forever be displayed under the title of your study sheet. :)
